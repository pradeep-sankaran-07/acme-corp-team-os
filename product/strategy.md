# Product and GTM strategy

## The wedge
Most tools can send sequences. The hard, unglamorous parts are handling replies well and protecting deliverability. That is where teams get burned and where Acme is best. Lead with the wedge: "your AI rep that handles replies and never burns your domain."

## Land and expand
- Land: one or two AI reps on a single, well-defined ICP, proving pipeline per rep.
- Expand: more reps (more ICPs and segments), then turn on LinkedIn multichannel and deeper CRM workflows.

## Where we invest
1. Reply handling quality (classification, objection handling, meeting-booking confidence).
2. Deliverability guardrails (deterministic sending limits, domain health).
3. Multichannel (LinkedIn) to lift reply rates.

## Principle that shapes the product
Outbound has parts that must be exact and parts that should flex. Sending limits, unsubscribe handling, and CRM writes are deterministic and hard-coded. Reply drafting and personalization are non-deterministic and governed by guardrails plus evals. See `decisions.md`.
