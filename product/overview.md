# Acme Outbound: product overview

The AI BDR platform. A revenue team configures AI reps that run outbound end to end.

## Modules
1. Lead Sourcing and Enrichment - define an ICP, find matching leads and accounts, enrich with firmographics and buying signals.
2. Sequencer - build multichannel sequences (email and LinkedIn) with AI personalization per lead.
3. Reply Handling - read inbound replies, classify them (interested, objection, out of office, unsubscribe), draft or send responses, and route to meeting booking.
4. Meeting Booking - turn an interested reply into a scheduled meeting on the right rep's calendar.
5. Deliverability - mailbox warmup, domain health monitoring, per-mailbox sending caps, and automatic pause on spam-rate spikes.
6. CRM Sync - two-way sync with Salesforce and HubSpot.
7. Analytics - pipeline sourced, reply and positive-reply rates, meetings booked, per rep and per sequence.

## How it is sold
Per AI rep (seat) plus usage (enrichments and sends). Land with one or two reps on a focused ICP, expand by seats and by turning on more modules.

## What makes it different
Reply handling quality and deliverability guardrails. See `strategy.md` and the PRDs in `prds/`.
