# Cobalt Security - deal brief (2026-06-18)

Prepared by Lena Vossen (AE). Example of the deal-brief skill output. Draft for internal use; review before sending externally.

## Dominant value angle
Deliverability and reply quality. Cobalt sells to regulated security and IT buyers where a single spam incident is costly, and they are frustrated by manual reply triage on their current Outreach plus Clay stack. Lead with "an AI rep that handles replies well and protects your domain," not raw autonomy or volume.

## 1. Situation
Cobalt runs outbound with Outreach plus Clay. Sourcing is fine, but reply handling is manual and deliverability is fragile. They want predictable pipeline for 3 AEs without growing an SDR team, targeting security and IT leaders.

## 2. Why Acme Outbound
- Full loop in one system: sourcing, sequencing, reply handling, meeting booking, Salesforce sync.
- Deliverability guardrails: per-mailbox deterministic caps, domain health monitoring, auto-pause on spam spikes.
- Reply quality: classification and objection handling inside guardrails, with meetings booked automatically.

## 3. Expected impact (illustrative, to confirm)
- Replace manual reply triage across 3 reps.
- Hold spam-complaint rate low while scaling volume.
- Predictable meetings per rep per month once warmed up.

## 4. Proposed scope and plan
- 3 AI reps, Salesforce two-way sync, email first, LinkedIn when the multichannel feature ships.
- Onboarding: ICP from closed-won data, mailbox warmup, first sequences, reply-handling content.
- Security review and a Salesforce technical session as part of onboarding.

## 5. Pricing
Per rep plus usage; figures to be confirmed in the formal quote.
