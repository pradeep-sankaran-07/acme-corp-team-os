# Glossary (outbound and BDR terms)

- BDR / SDR: Business / Sales Development Rep. The role that does outbound prospecting and books meetings. Acme Outbound provides AI reps that do this.
- ICP: Ideal Customer Profile. The firmographic and signal definition of who to target.
- Sequence / cadence: an ordered series of outbound touches (emails, LinkedIn steps) over days.
- Multichannel: mixing email and LinkedIn (and others) in one sequence.
- Enrichment: filling in lead and account data (title, company size, tech stack, signals).
- Deliverability: whether emails reach the inbox vs spam. Driven by domain health, warmup, sending volume, and content.
- Warmup: gradually ramping a mailbox's sending volume to build sender reputation.
- Reply handling: reading inbound replies, classifying them (interested, objection, out of office, unsubscribe), and responding.
- Meeting booking: converting an interested reply into a scheduled meeting on a rep's calendar.
- CRM sync: two-way data sync with Salesforce or HubSpot.
- Pipeline sourced: the value of opportunities created from Acme-sourced meetings.
- Reply rate / positive reply rate: replies (or positive replies) divided by messages sent.
- AI rep / seat: one configured AI SDR. Acme prices per rep plus usage.
