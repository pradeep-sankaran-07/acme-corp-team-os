# Market, ICP and competitors (dummy)

## Ideal customer profile
Mid-market B2B SaaS companies (50 to 500 employees) with an outbound motion and a revenue leader who owns pipeline. Signals: an existing SDR team or the intent to build one, a CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot) in place, a defined ICP, and meaningful outbound volume.

Buyer: VP Sales, CRO, or Head of Demand Gen. Champion is often a RevOps or growth lead.

## What they are trying to do
- Generate more qualified pipeline without linearly hiring SDRs.
- Make outbound messaging consistent and on-brand.
- Stop burning sending domains and landing in spam.

## Competitors (dummy framing)
- Full-stack AI SDR: 11x, Artisan. Compete on autonomy; Acme differentiates on reply quality and deliverability guardrails.
- Data and orchestration: Clay, Apollo. Strong on sourcing; Acme covers the full loop including replies and meetings.
- Sequencers: Outreach, Salesloft. Strong incumbents for human SDRs; Acme is AI-native and cheaper to scale.

## Positioning
Not "another send tool." Acme is the AI rep that handles the hard parts (replies, objections, deliverability) so pipeline is predictable. Land on the wedge, expand to the full outbound loop.
