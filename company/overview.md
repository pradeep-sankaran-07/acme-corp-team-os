# Acme Corp: overview

Fictional company for this demo.

## What Acme is
Acme Corp builds Acme Outbound, an AI BDR / outbound platform. Instead of hiring and ramping a team of SDRs, a revenue team deploys AI "reps" that source and enrich leads, run personalized multichannel sequences (email and LinkedIn), handle replies and objections, book qualified meetings, and sync everything to the CRM.

## The problem it solves
Outbound is expensive and slow to scale: SDRs are costly to hire and ramp, reply quality is inconsistent, deliverability is fragile, and pipeline is unpredictable. Acme Outbound turns outbound into software: predictable pipeline per AI rep, consistent messaging, and deliverability handled by the system.

## Stage and shape (dummy)
- Seed stage, about 2 years old, roughly 30 customers, 24 employees.
- Mostly mid-market B2B SaaS customers in North America and Europe.
- Land with one or two AI reps replacing or augmenting an SDR pod, expand by seats and modules.

## Business model
- Per-seat subscription (per AI rep) plus usage (enrichments and sends).
- Two-way CRM sync (Salesforce, HubSpot) keeps Acme as the outbound engine on top of the customer's system of record.

## Where Acme wins
Reply handling quality and deliverability. Many tools can send sequences; fewer handle replies well or protect the sending domain. That is the wedge. See `market.md` and `../product/strategy.md`.
