# learnings

Accumulated rules and patterns. Add here when a skill or routine should behave differently next time. Keep each entry short: the rule, and why.

- Resolve customers by email domain first, then fuzzy name. Skip personal domains so a private sender never becomes a new customer.
- Lead a deal-brief with one dominant value angle (pipeline volume, rep productivity, or deliverability and quality), not all three. Why: one clear angle lands better; the human picks it.
- Append, never overwrite, customer history. Refresh summary.md but keep calls/ and emails/ as a growing record.
- Keep customer-facing artifacts to a human review step before they are sent.
